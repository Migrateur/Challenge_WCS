About Us

Behind the Scenes - How is a WILD CIRCUS show created?

"Through creation, WILD CIRCUS is more than ever trying to push the boundaries of imagination, to amaze and continually surprise the viewer. Innovation and creativity have always been at the heart of our shows and will continue to transcend our future productions. "


Dream the unimaginable, create the unexpected

At WILD CIRCUS, dreaming is at the heart of our philosophy. To propel our adventures further and push the limits of our dreams - we believe in our people - they are the driving force of our business. At WILD CIRCUS, we offer our artists and creators all the freedom they need to bring their most unusual dreams to life. WILD CIRCUS's international headquarters in Montreal is home to nearly 400 artisans and specialists from all over the world who imagine and make more than 25,000 items for our shows around the world. Each production originates from the inspiration and ideas of a creative core comprised of a creative director, scriptwriter-director, choreographer, and costume, lighting and set designers.


Our artists

At the heart of the creative energy that drives all WILD CIRCUS shows are athletes, acrobats and artists at the top of their game. These men and women are driven by the passion to always go beyond their limits. Each artist is an integral part of the originality of WILD CIRCUS's productions, and his contribution is essential to the troupe's success.

  • More than 1,300 artists from around 55 different countries.
  • Each show has 50 to 100 artists.
  • 35% of them come from sports such as artistic, rhythmic and acrobatic gymnastics as well as trampoline, tumbling, diving, synchronized swimming and urban acrobatic disciplines (B-boy / hip-hop, urban movement, etc.).
  • 34% come from circus arts disciplines.
  • 31% come from various artistic backgrounds such as dance, music, singing, physical theater and street arts.
